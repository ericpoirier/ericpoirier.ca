I'm Eric Poirier, from Gatineau, Québec, Canada. I've been with the [Eclipse Foundation](https://eclipse.org) for 11 years — this March marked the anniversary — and I recently stepped into the role of Web Development Team Lead.

I spend most of my days working on the web infrastructure behind the Eclipse ecosystem: the foundation site, project sites, working group portals, and the various tools that keep it all running. It's a lot of Drupal, some Hugo, custom PHP, and lately a growing amount of AI tooling.
